Our Pillars

Professionalism

Deeply rooted in a culture of professionalism, Delta Sigma Pi offers an expansive alumni network in all industries from consulting to technology and provides its members with invaluable resources for career advancement.

Finance

Our Brothers are equipped with tools to succeed in the financial world through presentations and workshops on financial literacy, current events news, excel functions, and more. Each year, we model a $10,000 budget and function as a small business.

Community Service

Driven towards excellence, Deltasigs are leaders in their communities. Each year, our members give back through purposeful volunteer projects. Delta Sigma Pi is a socially responsible organization committed to changing the world.

Social

With over 260,000 members worldwide, Delta Sigma Pi brothers are like-minded individuals who support, motivate, and inspire each other. From studying to working out and everything in between, our community fosters a lifelong brotherhood.

About Nu Rho Chapter



Delta Sigma Pi History

Delta Sigma Pi is a professional fraternity organized to foster the study of business in universities; to encourage scholarship, social activity and the association of students for their mutual advancement by research and practice; to promote closer affiliation between the commercial world and students of commerce, and to further a higher standard of commercial ethics and culture and the civic and commercial welfare of the community.

Delta Sigma Pi was founded on November 7, 1907 at New York University at the School of Commerce, Accounts and Finance by Harold Valentine Jacobs, Alexander Frank Makay, Alfred Moysello, and Henry Albert Tienken. Our chapter, Nu Rho, was established at the University of California, Davis on December 3, 1994. Today, there are over 260,000 members and 287 chapters internationally.

Founded on a tradition of excellence, Delta Sigma Pi is the foremost co-ed professional fraternity for students pursuing careers in business.

Presidential Statement

The Nu Rho Chapter of Delta Sigma Pi provides a platform for UC Davis students to develop valuable professional skills while creating life-long friendships. Our Fraternity is dedicated to helping students realize their potential and succeed in the business world. As a Brother, you will have the opportunity to connect with like-minded business students and participate in an organization committed to advancing the business community at Davis. You will gain leaderships skills through organizing events and taking on roles within the Fraternity. You will refine your public speaking skills with workshops and presentations. You will discover new career interests and find mentors to help you get started. Most importantly, you will meet some of the most driven and compassionate people. And no matter where you are in life, your Delta Sigma Pi experience will shape your aspirations and provide you with the necessary tools to achieve them. This has certainly been true for me. Ask any of the brothers and alumni in our network, and they will tell you the same. So what are you waiting for? Success starts here.

Sincerely,

Discover Delta Sigma Pi

Delta Sigma Pi is a professional fraternity organized to foster the study of business in universities; to encourage scholarship, social activity and the association of students for their mutual advancement by research and practice; to promote closer affiliation between the commercial world and students of commerce, and to further a higher standard of commercial ethics and culture and the civic and commercial welfare of the community.
